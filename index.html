Progression Rules: Upper compounds +2.5kg when hitting top of rep range at RPE 7-8 for 2 sessions. Lower compounds +5kg same criteria. Accessories add reps before adding weight.

Fat Loss: The gym builds muscle. The kitchen drives fat loss. Moderate calorie deficit, 1.6-2g protein per kg bodyweight, 7-8 hours sleep.
